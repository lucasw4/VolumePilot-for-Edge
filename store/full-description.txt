VolumePilot for Edge gives you simple, precise audio control for tabs in your current Microsoft Edge window.

Features:
- Adjust volume from 0% to 200%
- Mute instantly and unmute quickly
- Half the current volume with one click
- Reset volume to 100% instantly
- Apply settings across tabs in the current window
- Auto-apply settings to newly opened tabs
- Save your last-used settings between sessions

VolumePilot is designed for fast, practical control without changing your OS-level Edge mixer volume.

Note:
- Some restricted pages (such as edge:// pages) cannot be controlled due to browser limitations.
